About V-Guard

V-Guard Industries Limited, established in 1977, is one of India's most trusted brands for home appliances and electrical products. With over 45 years of excellence, V-Guard has been protecting and empowering Indian homes.

From voltage stabilizers to water heaters, ceiling fans to water purifiers, V-Guard offers a comprehensive range of products that combine innovation with reliability. Our commitment to quality has made us a household name across India.

Headquartered in Kochi, Kerala, V-Guard operates with a nationwide network of dealers and service centers, ensuring that help is always nearby.
